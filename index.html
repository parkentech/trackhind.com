Government Approved Vehicle Tracking System (AIS-140)

The Requirement AIS-140 standard vehicle tracking system is Vehicle location tracking and Emergency Button for the public Transport vehicles. The AIS -140 Vehicle tracking system is component of intelligent transport system. Intelligent Transport System (ITS) is globally proven to optimize the utilization of existing transport infrastructure and improve transport system in terms of efficiency, quality, comfort and safety. Vehicle tracking system includes VTS device with Panic button and Backend control system.
